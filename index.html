Excellence in Eye Care Since 2008

At VisionCare Optics, we combine state-of-the-art technology with personalized care to provide comprehensive vision solutions. Our team of certified optometrists and optical technicians are committed to helping you achieve optimal vision health.

Founded by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, our clinic has grown from a small neighborhood practice to the region's leading optical center, serving over 25,000 satisfied customers with cutting-edge technology and compassionate care.
